PHOTOGRAPHS FOR ANY OCCASION

•MY EXPERIENCE•

Although I am currently attending the University of Ottawa as a digital journalism undergraduate student, I have educated myself extensively in the field of photography. I took a multimedia course that taught me the ins and outs of how to professionally adjust my settings, and everyday I make a point to enhance my adeptness in photojournalism in some way. I have done shoots for families, dogs, and weddings, and am constantly looking for new projects to take on. I also have had a photograph published by Esprit De Corps, a military magazine.
